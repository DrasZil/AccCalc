AccCalc v13.1.0 Documentation

AccCalc 13.1.0 is a focused academic-completion release for calculators, reviewer workspaces, lessons, quizzes, and integration across weak-but-core curriculum families.

New Calculators And Workspaces

  • Audit Materiality and Misstatement Planner
  • Income Tax Payable and Credits Review
  • Revenue Cycle Control Review
  • Fraud Risk Response Planner
  • Negotiable Instruments Issue Spotter
  • v13.1 Academic Review Support workpaper template

Learning And Practice Expansion

New original Study Hub topics cover audit materiality, tax payable, AIS revenue controls, RFBT negotiable instruments, fraud-risk response, and intermediate-accounting route discipline. Each topic includes quiz questions, worked examples, mistake warnings, scan signals, and linked calculator routes.

Curriculum Coverage Movement

  • Audit: stronger planning, materiality, and misstatement-response support.
  • Taxation: stronger final payable, credits, payments, and overpayment support.
  • AIS: stronger revenue-cycle and assertion-based control review.
  • Governance: stronger fraud-risk and escalation support.
  • RFBT: stronger negotiable-instrument issue spotting.
  • FAR: stronger intermediate-accounting route discipline.

Integration

The new routes are wired through app catalog/search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, Study Hub recommendations, related page menus, tests, and workpaper templates.
