# AccCalc v13.1.0 Release Notes

AccCalc `13.1.0` is the final high-value academic completion pass. It deliberately focuses on calculators, reviewer workspaces, Study Hub lessons, quizzes, and integration across weak-but-core curriculum families rather than shell redesign.

## Main Academic Additions

- Added Audit Materiality and Misstatement Planner for planning materiality, performance materiality, clearly trivial thresholds, aggregate misstatement pressure, and response guidance.
- Added Income Tax Payable and Credits Review for taxable income, gross tax due, withholding credits, quarterly payments, refundable credits, penalties, final payable, and overpayment.
- Added Revenue Cycle Control Review for AIS order-to-cash controls, assertion mapping, weak control gaps, walkthrough evidence, and audit follow-up.
- Added Fraud Risk Response Planner for fraud-triangle cues, management override, evidence-quality concerns, audit response, and governance escalation.
- Added Negotiable Instruments Issue Spotter for holder status, defenses, party liability, presentment, dishonor, and notice review.
- Added a v13.1 academic review support workpaper template for documenting issue, route, result, limitation, practice follow-up, and status.

## Learning And Practice

- Added original Study Hub lessons and quizzes for audit materiality, income-tax payable, AIS revenue controls, RFBT negotiable instruments, fraud-risk response, and intermediate-accounting route discipline.
- Practice questions emphasize route choice, plausible distractors, and feedback that sends students back into the right lesson or workspace.
- Intermediate-accounting support now reinforces recognition, measurement, presentation, disclosure, and schedule-building route discipline.

## Discovery And Integration

- App catalog/search surfaces all v13.1 routes by aliases, categories, keywords, and subject-family terms.
- Smart Solver recognizes audit materiality, tax payable, revenue-cycle controls, fraud-risk response, and negotiable-instrument prompts.
- OCR routing recommends the new pages for scanned notes, textbook-style prompts, and worked-solution language.
- Calculator page menus inherit related Study Hub lessons and quizzes through the route-to-topic link system.
- Workpaper Studio includes the v13.1 mixed academic review template.

## Academic Integrity

The release uses the user's curriculum and book-inspired subject families only as prioritization anchors. All explanations, worked examples, quiz items, and route descriptions are original.
