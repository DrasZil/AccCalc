# AccCalc 13.4.0 Release Notes

AccCalc 13.4.0 is a category-gap-closure and integrated-review release. It focuses on weaker curriculum families, complete topic loops, and CPA-style mixed review rather than scattered expansion.

## New Workspaces

- Audit Analytical Procedures Review: prior/current change, expected amount, tolerable difference, unexplained difference, excess difference, and risk-pressure follow-up.
- Tax Deduction Substantiation Review: claimed deductions, disallowed items, substantiation percentage, unsupported exposure, taxable income after review, and educational tax-effect triage.
- RFBT Security Contracts Remedy Review: obligation coverage, collateral value, priority claims, remedy cost, deficiency, recovery ratio, documentation strength, and remedy risk.
- CPA Integrated Review Studio: mixed-review readiness scoring for topic identification, computation accuracy, explanation quality, assumption discipline, and follow-up completion.

## Study And Practice

- Added original Study Hub lessons and quizzes for analytical procedures, deduction substantiation, security contracts/remedies, and integrated route repair.
- Quiz feedback teaches route choice, professional-boundary awareness, documentation discipline, and next-step repair.
- New topics link back to related workspaces, Smart Solver, workpapers, and older Study Hub review modules.

## Integrated Review

- Added a real mixed-review repair layer through the CPA Integrated Review Studio.
- The readiness model identifies the weakest step before the learner repeats another mixed set.
- The integrated review path connects Study Hub, Smart Solver, workpapers, and category-specific tools.

## Integration

- New routes are registered in app catalog/search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, Study Hub, related page menus, and workpapers.
- Added the `v13-4-category-gap-and-mixed-review` workpaper template for category-gap tracking and mixed-review repair.
- Added regression coverage for computation, search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, Study Hub topics, and workpaper registration.

## Boundaries

Audit, tax, and RFBT/law surfaces remain educational reviewer support. They are not current professional standards advice, tax authority guidance, legal advice, or engagement documentation. Users should verify real-world work against current official sources or qualified professionals.
