# AccCalc 13.3.0 Release Notes

AccCalc 13.3.0 is a super-completion release focused on curriculum coverage, reviewer depth, page clarity, and long-term survivability.

## New Workspaces

- AFAR Installment Sales Gross Profit Review: gross profit rate, realized gross profit, deferred gross profit, and repossession recovery signals.
- Audit Going Concern Review Workspace: adverse conditions, management plans, evidence support, residual doubt, and completion-stage procedure direction.
- Tax Remedy Timeline Review: educational-only remedy triage using entered deadline pressure, evidence completeness, materiality, and procedural complexity.
- AIS Incident Response Triage: confidentiality, integrity, availability, containment, evidence readiness, residual incident risk, and response tier.

## Study And Practice

- Added original lessons and quizzes for installment sales, going concern, tax remedies, incident response, FAR disclosure, bookkeeping closing/reversing, RFBT corporation lifecycle, and integrated CPA case routing.
- Each new lesson links into related tools, quizzes, and follow-up routes.
- Quiz explanations emphasize route choice, assumptions, and mistake repair.

## Integration

- New routes are wired into app catalog/search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, Study Hub, related page menus, and workpapers.
- Added the `v13-3-super-completion-review` workpaper template for learn-practice-solve-review tracking.
- Added regression coverage for computation, search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, study topics, and workpaper template registration.

## UX Clarity

- Calculator and reviewer pages now show a compact first-screen strip:
  - when to use the page
  - what output to expect
  - where to continue after solving

## Boundaries

Tax, audit, legal/RFBT, governance, and AIS content remains educational support only. Users should verify current professional, legal, tax, and standards requirements from official sources or qualified advisers.
