AccCalc v12.4.0

Compact Personalization And Academic Expansion

Version 12.4.0 keeps Appearance compact and polished while expanding calculators, study topics, practice support, and the learn-practice-solve loop.

Main Changes

  • Added Audit Misstatement Evaluation Workspace, Segregation of Duties Conflict Matrix, and Governance Escalation Planner.
  • Added new study and quiz coverage for receivables estimation, cash discounts, cost behavior, and margin of safety.
  • Updated Study Hub, Practice Hub, and quiz results so lessons, quizzes, and tools link together more directly.
  • Kept Settings Appearance compact while preserving familiar themes and extending the palette families.

Academic Depth

New weak-track tools

Audit, AIS, and Governance now have more practical classroom workspaces instead of relying on generic reviewer surfaces alone.

Tighter study loop

Students can move from lesson to quiz to calculator faster, and weak quiz topics now have clearer return paths.

Theme And Appearance

  • Appearance stays summary-first with quick family switching and a discoverable gallery.
  • Classic, Ocean, Slate, Rose, Blossom, Lavender, and Emerald remain available.
  • Butter, Moss, Palm, Guava, Sunset, Sangria, Seabreeze, Lagoon, and Odyssey extend the palette.
  • Theme state still applies before React mounts to reduce flicker.
