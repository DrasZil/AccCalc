AccCalc v13.4.0 Documentation

Category gap closure and integrated CPA-style review release.

AccCalc 13.4.0 closes weaker academic tracks with four connected additions: audit analytical procedures, tax deduction substantiation, RFBT security-contract remedies, and a CPA Integrated Review Studio. Each addition is wired into lessons, quizzes, search, Smart Solver, OCR routing, and workpapers.

New Workspaces

Audit Analytical Procedures ReviewCompares prior, current, and expected amounts to surface unexplained differences, tolerable thresholds, and follow-up pressure.
Tax Deduction Substantiation ReviewSeparates disallowed items, supported deductions, unsupported exposure, taxable income after review, and educational tax-effect triage.
RFBT Security Contracts Remedy ReviewReviews collateral coverage, priority claims, remedy cost, deficiency, recovery ratio, and documentation strength.
CPA Integrated Review StudioScores mixed-review readiness across topic identification, computation, explanation, assumptions, and follow-up completion.

Study And Practice

  • New original Study Hub topics live in src/features/study/studyExpansion1340.ts.
  • Each topic includes reviewer notes, worked examples, checkpoints, and guided quiz feedback.
  • Practice items push learners back to the closest lesson, workspace, or integrated review route after a miss.

Integration

  • Routes are registered in src/App.tsx and src/utils/appCatalog.ts.
  • Smart Solver mappings live in src/features/smart/smartSolver.engine.ts.
  • OCR routing lives in src/features/scan-check/services/ocr/ocrRouting.ts.
  • The v13.4 workpaper template lives in src/features/workpapers/workpaperTemplates.ts.

Educational Boundary

Audit, tax, RFBT/law, governance, AIS, and integrated-review pages are educational reviewer support only. They are not official legal, tax, professional standards, audit engagement, or security guidance.
